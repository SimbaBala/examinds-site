Privacy Policy

TabWell for macOS

Effective date: June 22, 2026

No data collected

TabWell does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or usage information. The app has no network access and contains no analytics, telemetry, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

What stays on your device

All files you open or create in TabWell remain entirely on your Mac. TabWell stores only two things locally:

Preferences — your editor settings (font, theme, etc.) are saved to your Mac's standard user preferences store (UserDefaults). They never leave your device.

Security-scoped bookmarks — to reopen files across sessions without requesting permission each time, TabWell stores Apple sandbox bookmarks in your app's sandboxed container. These bookmarks are opaque tokens managed by macOS; they contain no readable file paths and cannot be read by any other app.

Sandbox

TabWell is fully sandboxed for the Mac App Store. It operates with the minimum entitlements required to function: access to user-selected files (read/write) and app-scoped bookmarks. It has no network entitlement and cannot make outbound connections.

Third parties

TabWell does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services. No data is shared with any third party because no data is collected in the first place.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes in a material way, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. We will not collect any data without clearly disclosing it here first.
